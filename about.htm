About Us

Our Story

Frond is an online plant store founded in 2021. Frond aims to deliver healthy, sustainably-sourced indoor plants across the nation. We are a small team of plant-lovers based in Pretoria. We put great care into our website, our plants and our delivery service. We love our work and we hope you will too!
